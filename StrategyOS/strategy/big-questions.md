## Guidelines

**What makes a good Big Question:**
- Fundamental to strategic direction
- Affects multiple cascade elements  
- Requires judgment, not just data
- Worth extended exploration

**Keep it short:** 3-7 active Big Questions maximum. More than that, some are probably hypotheses or sub-questions.
